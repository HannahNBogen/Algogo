====================== 
HOW TO GET A MAC APP ? 
====================== 
 
1. Get a JAR (either you compile it or you download it). 
2. Download jar2app (https://github.com/Jorl17/jar2app). 
3. Run the following command in the jar2app folder : 
   jar2app "<Location of the JAR>" "<Location of the APP>" -b "xyz.algogo.desktop.AlgogoDesktop" -d "Algogo Desktop" -n "Algogo Desktop v1.0" -i "icon.icns" -v "v1.0" -c "This product is licensed under GNU GPL v3. Please check https://github.com/Skyost/Algogo/blob/master/LICENSE.md for more informations."
